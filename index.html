About Us

Mask Up: Stay Anonymous is a platform designed to provide a safe and anonymous space for students and young adults to share their emotional struggles and receive peer support.

Community-Based Support

Daily prompts for mood tracking, with access to supportive messages, mental health tips, or options to connect with peers feeling similarly.

All discussions are monitored to ensure they remain supportive and non-triggering, maintaining a positive atmosphere.
